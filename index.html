Frequently Asked Questions

Is this safe for my Robux?

Yes. We use an **Escrow system**. You pay our Ro-Pay system, and we hold the funds. The Creator is only paid from our system once they mark the promotion as complete.

How do I verify as a creator?

Log in to the dashboard and click "Become Creator". You'll submit an application with your channel links and stats. Our admin team reviews every application.

What if a creator scams me?

Funds are held in **Escrow**. The creator isn't paid until they deliver the work. If there is a dispute, you can contact our support team. We can review the chat logs and promotion details to issue a refund if necessary.
